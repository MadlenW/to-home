Towards Home

The card set is a tool for conversation and reflection. Organised across three temporal decks: Past, Present and Future. The questions invite you to map how your relationship to home has been shaped, what it looks like now, and what you might still be reaching for. Use them alone or with others, in any order, at any pace.

Download print file

A4 format. Print double-sided on 300gsm cardstock, flip on the long edge. Cut along the crop marks. A home printer works fine.
